Revenue Management

The art and science of having the optimal portfolio mix at the right price and discount levels that reflect your brand equity, competitive advantage, and shopper behavior.

Recent global trends in raw material costs, fast innovation by historically low-cost brands and growth of digital commerce platforms have challenged traditional methods of revenue management at large organizations, highlighting the urgent need for innovation in this field.

Strengthen the pillars of your
Revenue Management Function

Strategic Pricing
Evaluate whether your price helps or kills your assortment performance. Using your competitive analysis, this insight fits in perfect for a future game.
Promotion Optimization
Determine your historic base sales and analyze price elasticities to understand promotion cannibalization and sales trends to accurately plan future promotions.
Mix Management
Decide your future portfolio architecture to complement each other and thrive competitively, by allocating the individual brands into the right roles.
Price Pack Architecture
Drive your brand's strategic differentiation via pack size, tracking performance relative to their individual TDPs.
Scenario Planning
Initiate what-if scenario planning. Run logic of different scenarios, map them to their respective measures and use the added logic to foresee future sales/projections.
